How to Use the Application

      Inputting Ideas:

      • If you want to submit an idea for town improvements, click on the "Input Ideas" option in the navigation menu.

      • You will be directed to a page where you can enter your idea. Fill out any required information.

      • After completing the idea submission form, click a "Submit".

      Viewing Ideas:

      • To view ideas submitted by others, click on the "Display Ideas" option in the navigation menu.

      • Select the town for which you would like to see the ideas.

      • You will then see a list of top 3 ideas under each department.
